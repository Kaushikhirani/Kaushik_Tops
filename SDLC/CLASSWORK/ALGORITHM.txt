Algorithm: it is a process which is followed step by step.

an algorithm to make tea
step 1; start the gas.
step2; take an input (bowl,milk,water,tea,etc.)
step3; Boiling
step4; if tea is good, then goto next step,else go to step 1.
step5; output(serve)
step6; end.
